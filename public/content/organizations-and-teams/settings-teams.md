## Teams Settings

The Teams settings tab provides a centralized view of all teams within your organization. From here, you can see team member counts and delete teams that are no longer needed.

<Image src="https://storage.googleapis.com/hashnode_product_documentation_assets/organizations-and-teams/organization-settings/teams_settings.png" alt="" align="center" fullwidth="true" />

> Teams settings tab showing list of teams with member counts and delete options

## Accessing Teams Settings

Navigate to the Teams settings tab to view and manage all organization teams. This provides a centralized view of all teams in your organization.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    From your *organization overview* page, click `Settings` in the sidebar navigation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Teams Tab">
    Click the `Teams` tab at the top of the *Settings* page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Teams

See all teams in your organization along with their member counts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Team List">
    The *Teams settings* page displays all teams in your organization. Each *team* card shows:
    * Team name
    * Number of members in the team
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Member Counts">
    Use the member count information to understand team sizes and organization structure at a glance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting Teams

Remove teams that are no longer needed from your organization.

<Image src="https://storage.googleapis.com/hashnode_product_documentation_assets/organizations-and-teams/organization-settings/deleting_team.png" alt="" align="center" fullwidth="true" />

> Team deletion interface showing delete button and confirmation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Team">
    Find the team you want to delete in the *teams* list on the *Teams settings* page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Delete Team">
    Click the red trash can icon on the right side of the *team* card to delete the team.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm the deletion. The team and all its associated data will be permanently removed from your organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Note: Deleting a team will remove all team updates and team-specific content. Team members will remain in the organization but will no longer be associated with the deleted team.
</Callout>
